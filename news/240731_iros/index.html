I will be in IROS 2024

Created in July 31, 2024

2024

I will attend IROS 2024 in Abu Dhabi, UAE and will present the following conference papers:

  • Verdoja, Kucner, and Kyrki, “Bayesian Floor Field: Transferring People Flow Predictions Across Environments.”
  • Chaubey, Verdoja, and Kyrki, “Jointly Learning Cost and Constraints from Demonstrations for Safe Trajectory Generation.”

Moreover, I am part of the organizing committees of the following workshops:

  • From Learning-based to Foundation Models for Mapping: Challenges and Opportunities
